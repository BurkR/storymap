Bend

Vaccinium membranaceum and blue (Cascade) huckleberry V. deliciosum are among several varieties of huckleberry that grow in Western Oregon.

Indigenous peoples harvested great quantities of these berries and dried them with fire which provided a sweet treat throughout the winter.
